At the Start of My Hymn


At the start of my hymn : I glorify God’s name
With joy I exalt Him : Pikhristos we proclaim
Was born in Bethlehem : On the cross, He was slain
He rose on the third day : And to His mother we say

For B the bush burning : Aflame but unconsumed
The divine fire didn’t : Cause damage or burn you
Through the Holy birthing : Of the Blessed One in Truth
You kept virginity : Mother of purity

For C the censer gold : Gold is for purity
You the censer behold : The live coal you carried
Symbolising the Lord : Fulfilling prophecies
They spoke of the mystery : Ethve pijinmisi
